Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct pt::memory::released_buffer< T >

A lightweight Plain Old Data like structure returned by release() carrying the raw pointer, size, deleter and location. Ownership of the memory (and the responsibility to call the deleter) transfers to the receiver.

returned by release() of smart smart_buffers
